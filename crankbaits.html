Lure Guide Details

Crankbaits

A search bait designed to imitate baitfish and cover water at different depths.

← Back to Lure Guide

About This Lure

Crankbaits are hard-bodied lures designed to dive, wobble, and imitate baitfish. They are useful when anglers want to cover water and locate active fish.

Different crankbaits run at different depths, so they can be used in shallow ponds, along rocky banks, around points, and near deeper structure. They are especially useful when fish are feeding on baitfish.

Best Tip: Try to make the crankbait bump into cover or bottom structure. Many bites happen right after the lure deflects.

Quick Facts

Best For Bass, Crappie
Skill Level Intermediate
Best Season Spring-Fall
Best Use Covering Water

Best Retrieve

Use a steady retrieve with pauses, speed changes, or contact with structure.

Where to Use

Fish them around points, rocky banks, docks, drop-offs, and open-water structure.

Best Colors

Shad, bluegill, crawfish, chartreuse, and natural baitfish colors work well.
