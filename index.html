Seasonal Fishing Tips

Fishing conditions change throughout the year. Use seasonal patterns, weather, and water temperature to choose better spots and lures.

Early Morning

Fish shallow areas before the sun gets high.

Shaded Banks

Target docks, trees, rocks, and overhanging cover.

Slow Retrieval

Use a slower reel speed when fish are less active in hot or cold water.
