Lure Guide Details

Soft Plastics

One of the most versatile lure types for bass fishing in ponds, lakes, and reservoirs.

← Back to Lure Guide

About This Lure

Soft plastics are one of the most dependable lure choices for freshwater fishing. They are especially popular for bass because they can imitate worms, crawfish, minnows, and other natural prey.

These lures work well because they can be fished slowly, which helps when fish are less aggressive. They are also useful around cover like docks, weeds, laydowns, and shallow banks.

Best Tip: Fish soft plastics slowly. Let the bait fall, pause, then lightly twitch it to create a natural movement.

Quick Facts

Best For Bass
Skill Level Beginner-Advanced
Best Season Spring-Fall
Best Water Clear or Stained

Best Rigging Options

Texas rig, wacky rig, Carolina rig, and weightless rig setups all work well.

Where to Use

Fish them around docks, grass lines, fallen trees, shallow banks, and pond edges.

Best Colors

Green pumpkin, watermelon red, black, blue, and natural baitfish colors.
